## Exclusions

The following services, products or conditions are **NOT covered**:

### Cosmetic & Elective Procedures
1. Plastic / Cosmetic surgery including double eyelids, acne, keloids, scars, skin tags, gynaecomastia, diffused alopecia / hair loss, etc.
2. Corrective treatment for refractive errors including Orthoptics, Visual stimulation, Radial Keratotomy, Lasik, Intralase, Xyoptics, phacik IOL implant
3. All corrective glasses or contact lenses (except monofocal intraocular lenses in cataract surgery)

### Experimental & Unproven Treatments
2. Experimental, investigative or unproven services not according to accepted professional standards:
   - Stem cell treatment and related workout
   - Blood surety
   - Hormone therapy and hormone replacement therapy (except for surgically induced menopause)
   - Surgical treatment for weight reduction or gain

### Behavioural & Self-Harm Related
3. Treatment for injuries from crime, felony, or while under influence of alcohol/narcotics/mind-altering substances
4. Suicide, attempted suicide, or intentionally self-inflicted injury
5. Smoking cessation programs and treatment for substance abuse (alcohol, narcotics, etc.)

### Care & Custodial Services
5. Private nursing care, custodial care in any setting, house calls, or rest cure services

### Reproduction & Sexual Health
6. Mechanical or chemical contraceptive methods or treatment for infertility
7. Sexual dysfunction, impotence tests/treatment, or sterilization
8. Pregnancy investigation and treatment including childbirth, Ectopic Pregnancy, Vesicular Mole
   - *Exception:* Miscarriage below 28 weeks due to accidental causes may be covered under Basic Policy
9. Sex transformation surgery and related hormone therapy
10. Circumcision (unless medically necessary for disease treatment)

### Infectious & Communicable Diseases
10. Sexually transmitted diseases, AIDS, AIDS Related Complex, communicable diseases requiring quarantine

### Alternative Therapies
11. Acupuncture, Acupressure, Chiropractic, Osteopathy, Reflexology, Bonesetting, Massage, Aroma Therapy, Herbal, Podiatric, Dietetic consultation, Traditional Complimentary Medicine

### Vitamins, Supplements & Over-Counter Products
12. Vitamins / Supplements, Herbal Cures, Anti Obesity / Weight Reducing Agents, Eye Lubricants, over the counter purchases
13. Soaps, Shampoos, Cleansers, Vitamin Creams, Vitamin Ointment, Moisturizers, Lubricants, Anti-Aging, Fairness Treatment

### Mental Health & Neurological
14. Psychotic, mental or nervous disorders, behavioral conditions, neurosis and their physiological or psychosomatic manifestations

### Congenital & Developmental
15. Treatment or assessment for congenital, hereditary or developmental ailments, deformities, disability or complications (dermoid cysts, childhood hernias, clubfoot, VSD, ASD, Thalassemia, Squint, Haemangioma)
16. Diseases or disabilities of newborn child contracted prior to, during, or within first 14 days of birth

### Diagnostic & Preventive
17. Allergy testing - blood / topical including patch test
18. Hospitalisation primarily for investigatory purpose, routine physical examinations, health check-ups, preventive treatments

### Rehabilitation & Therapy
19. Speech and Occupational therapy (unless part of rehabilitation program following hospitalization due to trauma)
27. Out-Patient physical therapy or physiotherapy not covered (only covered when referred by Specialist and provided by registered physiotherapist)

### Vision Correction
20. Corrective treatment for refractive errors (Orthoptics, Visual stimulation, Radial Keratotomy, Lasik, Intralase, Xyoptics, phacik IOL implant)
21. Corrective glasses or contact lenses (except monofocal intraocular lenses in cataract surgery)

### Dental
22. Dental care / treatment or oral surgery (except for accidental injuries)
    - Upper and lower jawbone surgery (except for direct treatment of acute traumatic injury or cancer)
    - Replacement of natural teeth, denture, bridges & crowns (except for accidental injury cases)

### Medical Devices & Equipment
23. Use or acquisition of appliances (artificial limbs, hearing aids, aero chambers, nebulising equipment, CPAP, CAPD, orthopedic pads)

### Environmental & War-Related
24. Effects from radiation or contamination by radioactivity
25. War, riot, rebellions, insurrection, civil commotion, explosion of war weapons, terrorism, active duty in armed forces, nuclear war, biological/chemical warfare

### Hospital Services & Non-Medical Items
26. Non-medical services (television, telephone, fax, radio, admission kit/pack, registration fees except during hospitalization)

### Specialized Treatments
28. Out-Patient rehabilitation therapy, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, photodynamic therapy, kidney dialysis (unless member has basic Group Policy coverage)

### Vaccinations & Preventive
29. Preventive vaccinations (except those per Ministry of Health Malaysia guidelines for eligible children only)

### Organ Donation & Transplant
30. Expenses for body organ donation and costs of organ acquisition

### Sleep Disorders
31. Investigation and treatment of sleep and snoring disorders

### Contact Lens & Cosmetic
32. Expenses for contact lens, cosmetic topically / orally / surgical procedures

## AIA Procedures

### Going to AIA Panel Clinic

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Check Coverage**
   - Member logs in to AIA+ App to check benefit coverage and utilization

2. **Find Clinic**
   - Member finds panel clinic through map locator

3. **Pre-Register**
   - Member clicks on 'Pre-register Visit' and clicks on 'Pre-Register' clinic via AIA+ App for verification (Within 500m Radius)

4. **Arrive at Clinic**
   - Member arrives at the panel clinic
   - Member shows eMedical card at registration counter

5. **Treatment**
   - Member gets treatment from general practitioner
   - GP issues e-Referral when necessary (for specialist / hospital)

6. **Bill Notification**
   - Member is informed of itemised bill through AIA+ App notification

---

### Going to Panel Specialist (Guarantee Letter via AIA+ APP)

**Important:** The Specialist will bill AIA directly for all covered services.

**Panel GP Referral Letter Validity:** 30 days

**Process:**

1. **Apply Guarantee Letter**
   - Member/Dependants apply Guarantee Letter via AIA+ APP at least one (1) day before appointment

2. **First Visit Documentation**
   - Submit Panel GP referral letter
   - Indicate diagnosis

3. **Subsequent Visits**
   - Submit appointment card

4. **Approval Process**
   - E-LoG (Electronic Letter of Guarantee) will be processed
   - When approved, member will receive pre-approved notification via MyAIA App
   - Member shows pre-approved notification at hospital registration counter
   - Hospital registration counter will activate GL

**Notes:**
- Hospital can assist to apply Specialist GL upon your visitation to Panel Specialist
- If visiting non-panel Specialist, you can pay first and submit claim for review

---

### Admission to Panel Hospital

**Important:** The Hospital will bill AIA directly for all covered services.

**6-Step Hospital Admission Process:**

**STEP 01: SPECIALIST**
- Specialist advice for admission & Member to present E-Medical card, NRIC or Passport for registration

**STEP 02: HOSPITAL**
- Doctor/Specialist sends Hospital Admission Form (HAF) to Call Centre for Guarantee Letter (GL) issuance

**STEP 03: CALL CENTRE**
- Guarantee Letter (GL) issued to panel hospital within 30 minutes after received complete documents from hospital

**STEP 04: TREATMENT**
- Member shows pre-approved notification at hospital registration counter
- Member admits to hospital and pays excess deposit (if requested by hospital)

**STEP 05: DISCHARGE**
- Hospital compiles all medical expenses incurred and fax to Call Centre for Final Guarantee Letter (GL) assessment

**STEP 06: END**
- Patient to pay excess/non-covered charges to hospital (if any)

## Claims Submission

### Claim Requirements

#### Documentation Needed
- Complete and sign the AIA Corporate Solutions Claim Form (available from HR Department or via AIA+ mobile app)
- **Panel GP referral letter** (for Specialist – first visit)
- **Original Receipts**
- **Copy of IC/Passport**
- **Appointment Card** (for follow-up)
- **Medical Report** (if any)
- **Lab Report** (if any)
- **Itemised Bill** with breakdown of charges

#### Medical Report Requirements
- **GP claim exceeds RM80:** Medical report required
- **Specialist claim exceeds RM150:** Medical report required
- **Hospital claim exceeds RM500:** Medical report required
- **Government hospital:** Discharge note required

### Submission Process

1. Complete and sign the AIA Corporate Solutions Claim Form
2. Attach all required documents listed above
3. Send completed claim form with attached documents to your Human Resources Department
4. **Processing Time:** Eligible claims with full and complete documents will be reimbursed within 21 days from the date of receipt by AIA Bhd.

### Claim Deadline

**All claims must be submitted to AIA Bhd. within 30 days from the date of treatment.**

---

### Types of Claims

#### Reimbursable Claims
1. Non-Panel Emergency Claims
2. Emergency Outpatient Treatment at Hospital Accident & Emergency Unit
3. Outpatient Government Hospital treatment

#### Non-Reimbursable Claims
1. Panel Clinic Claims without use of AIA E-Medical Card
2. Non-covered or excluded services, treatments and medication
3. Claims from non-panel Clinics in a non-emergency situation

## Emergency Guidelines

### Definition of Emergency

Emergency means treatment needed when immediate medical attention is required **within 12 hours** of injury, illness or symptoms which are sudden and severe, failing which the member's life could be threatened (e.g., accident and heart attack), or lead to significant deterioration of health.

### Emergency Procedure

1. **Seek Immediate Care**
   - Go to nearest GP Clinic or Accident and Emergency Unit (A&E) of nearest Hospital
   - **Note:** Direct specialist visit is NOT considered emergency

2. **Pay & Submit Claim**
   - Pay for treatment
   - Submit claim to AIA for review
   - In the Claim Form/receipt, indicate:
     - It was an emergency case
     - Date and time of visit
     - Reason for visit

3. **If Admitted to Hospital**
   - Call AIA 24-hour Call Centre at **1300 8888 60/70** for Letter Of Guarantee (LOG)
   - Or get hospital staff to assist with GL application

---

### Non-Cashless Specialist

**Important:** AIA's Letter of Guarantee (LOG) will NOT be issued to Non-Cashless Specialist / Medical Centers.

**If seeking consultation at Non-Cashless Specialists / Medical Centers:**
- Pay first
- Submit claim for review and reimbursement
- Claims reimbursed according to MMA Schedule of Fees and your benefit entitlement

## Important Notes & General Information

### Policy Information
1. The insurance risk is underwritten by AIA Bhd.
2. Any medical costs in excess of the benefit limit provided by your company will be borne by you.
3. Claims must be submitted to AIA within 30 days from the date of consultation or service.

### Medication Guidelines
4. Long term medications will only be issued on a monthly basis (Note: over the counter purchase – NOT covered)

### Regulatory Compliance
5. All medical costs under all insurance policies in Malaysia are subject to Malaysia Medical Association's Schedule 13 (KKM - Kementerian Kesihatan Malaysia) guideline.

### Coverage Exclusions
6. Any medical diagnosis or treatment that falls under policy's exclusion will not be covered.

### Hospital Deposits
7. Non-AIA platinum hospitals will collect admission deposit upfront upon hospital admission.

### Record Keeping
- You are advised to keep a copy of all documents sent to AIA Bhd.

### Contact Information
**For inquiries, contact your Human Resources Department**
