## üé§ Script & Delivery Notes

### **Opening Energy:**
Start with high energy and audience engagement. The hand-raising question immediately involves everyone and establishes shared pain points.

### **Demonstration Guidelines:**
- **Practice the demo** multiple times beforehand
- Have **backup screenshots** ready for technical issues
- Keep the demo **focused and fast-paced**
- **Narrate what's happening** so audience follows along
- **Highlight the "wow moments"** when AI extracts data

### **Data Presentation:**
- Use **visual charts** for impact metrics
- **Round numbers** for easier comprehension (75% vs 74.3%)
- **Connect metrics to business value** (time savings = cost savings)
- **Emphasize most compelling figures** with voice inflection

### **Handling Objections:**

**"Is this secure enough for employee financial data?"**
*"Security was our top priority. ClaimEase uses bank-level encryption, email-scoped access controls, and maintains complete audit trails. Every employee can only access their own data, and all activities are logged for compliance."*

**"How long will employees take to learn this?"**
*"That's the beauty of good design - it's intuitive. In our pilot testing, employees were successfully submitting claims within minutes of first login. The AI assistant guides them through any questions."*

**"What if the AI makes mistakes?"**
*"The AI provides suggestions that employees review and confirm. Every claim goes through the same approval workflow, but with pre-validated data. We've seen 90% fewer errors compared to manual entry."*

**"What's the ongoing cost?"**
*"Monthly operational costs are approximately $800, but we're saving $6,250 monthly through efficiency gains. The system pays for itself many times over."*
