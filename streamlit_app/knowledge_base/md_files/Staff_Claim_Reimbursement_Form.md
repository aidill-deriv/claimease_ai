## Important Notes & Guidelines

### Submission Rules

1. **One form per claim type:** You can only submit one form per claim type
   - Example: If you choose "Employee Benefit", you can submit a maximum of 10 entries under this type (e.g., Dental, Optical, etc.)

2. **Number of entries:** Specify the number of claim entries for your selected claim type
   - This will expand the form to allow for submission
   - **Maximum of 10 entries per form submission**

3. **Maximum receipts:** You can claim a maximum of 10 receipts per form submission

### Reference Information

To understand your benefits and entitlements:
- Go to **Sage People** → **General Information** → **Employee Benefits and Handbook** section

To obtain your **Unique ID**:
- Go to **Sage People** → **Work Details** → **Unique Id**

To obtain your **Department**:
- Go to **Sage People** → **Work Details Section** → **Department @ Team Level 1**

### E-Invoicing Implementation

**Applicable to Malaysia staff only**

We are implementing e-invoicing for all types of claims **EXCEPT for Employee Benefit claims**.

**Action Required:**
- Kindly request for an **e-invoice with our company details** from the supplier
- For more information, please refer to the email titled: **[Announcement] Changes to Staff Claim Process - E-Invoicing Implementation**

**Exception:** Employee Benefit claims (Dental, Optical, Health Screening) do NOT require e-invoicing

## Step-by-Step Submission Process

### Before You Submit

1. **Gather all documents:**
   - Clear image of receipt(s)/invoice(s) with your name
   - Approval from authorised person
   - Any supporting documents specific to your claim type

2. **Identify your claim type:**
   - Review the 5 claim types above
   - Ensure your claim falls under one category

3. **Get required information from Sage People:**
   - Your Unique ID (Work Details section)
   - Your Department (Work Details > Team Level 1)

### During Submission

1. **Fill in personal information:**
   - Full name, Unique ID, Department, Email, Location, Hiring Company

2. **Select your claim type:**
   - Choose from the 5 types

3. **Specify number of entries:**
   - Indicate how many receipts you're claiming (max 10)

4. **Fill in claim details:**
   - Total amount (sum of all entries)
   - Local currency
   - Headcount (if team building/lunch claim)

5. **Attach supporting documents:**
   - Receipt(s)/invoice(s)
   - Approval conversation
   - Relevant supporting documents
   - Headcount form (if applicable)
   - Certificate of completion (if educational)
   - Proposal form (if team building)

### After Submission

- Your claim will be reviewed by the appropriate department
- Processing timeline depends on claim type and completeness of documents
- Reference your claim details if you need to follow up
